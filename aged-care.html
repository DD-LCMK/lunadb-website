Specialized Capabilities for Healthcare

We build custom logic to handle the unique logistical challenges of patient care and staff management.

Smart Scheduling & Routing

We integrate the Google Maps API directly into your scheduling system. The database automatically calculates driving distances between patient visits, drastically reducing manual scheduling errors and providing immediate, highly accurate travel cost calculations for your staff.

Automated Roster Alarms

Never miss a patient visit. Our systems feature trigger-based logic that monitors upcoming schedules and issues automated alerts for patient roster updates, ensuring your care workers are always deployed exactly when and where they are needed.

Auto-Emailing Systems

Keep everyone in the loop without lifting a finger. The database automatically generates and dispatches secure, formatted emails to both customers (patients/families) and staff members regarding upcoming schedules and important updates.

Zero-Touch Invoicing

Convert completed staff schedules directly into billable invoices. Our Python-driven integration automatically pulls shift data, applies specific medical or care codes, and generates accurate, ready-to-send PDF invoices and automated documentation.

Staff Expense Analysis

Gain total visibility over operational costs. We build customized analytics dashboards that track and categorize business expenses, travel costs, and billable hours on a per-staff basis, providing management with real-time financial oversight.

Compliance & Documentation

Medical data requires absolute security. We establish centralized, permission-based systems to house compliance documentation, triggering automatic warnings before certifications expire and instantly generating required compliance forms on demand.
